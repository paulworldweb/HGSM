ABOUT US

About Hold God Strong Ministries

Hold God Strong Ministries is a growing faith-based ministry committed to sharing the Word of God, encouraging believers, and helping people grow stronger in prayer, faith, and spiritual understanding.

WHO WE ARE

A Ministry Focused on Faith and Growth

Our ministry exists to help people stay connected to God through prayer, worship, biblical teaching, and encouragement. We believe that every person can grow deeper in their walk with God when they are supported with truth, prayer, and spiritual guidance.

Through messages, online videos, prayer support, and ministry events, Hold God Strong Ministries seeks to reach people wherever they are and remind them to remain strong in the Lord.

🎯

Our Mission

To spread the Word of God, strengthen faith, and provide prayerful support that helps people remain grounded in Christ.

🌍

Our Vision

To build a ministry that reaches lives across communities through teaching, prayer, digital outreach, and spiritual encouragement.

❤️

Our Values

Faith, prayer, truth, compassion, consistency, and a commitment to helping people grow in their relationship with God.
